Mission

Our work is driven by a passionate belief that we all have a responsibility to protect orphan children who have physical disabilities by giving them shelter, food and clothing. We believe in nursery and primary education for them to help them achieving their full physical, intellectual, social and economic potential as individuals and responsible citizens.

Vision

The NGO aims to transform the lives of orphans who are disabled and help them pursue their dreams. We value integrity, excellence, vitality, collaboration, empowerment, commitment, dignity, equality and charity.
