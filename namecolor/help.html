Help

/namecolor
Syntax:
/namecolor <color> <style1> <style2> <player>

Color:
All Bukkit color codes are supported.
RGB color codes are supported as well, formatted #<RGB>

Style:
You can use as many of these styles as you want:
  • Bold
  • Underline
  • Italic
  • Magic
  • Strikethrough
These are available through Bukkit codes.
